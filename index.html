About Us

Our story goes way back…

All the way back to 2006 when we were both in high school and had no idea what the future would have in store for us. When Frank popped the question this past June, it actually wasn’t the first time he got down on one knee — that was back in 2009 when he asked Jourdyn to be his date to the senior prom.

A friendship began to blossom in those hallways, and it continued to grow stronger as the years passed, but timing just never seemed to be on our side. Eventually, a move to the West Coast and job in New Jersey put nearly three-thousand miles between us and it seemed like that might be the end of this friendship we had both cherished for so long.

But then, after living our lives apart for about three years, the silence was finally broken and that friendship was rekindled. The rest, as they say, was history. We spent our first official year together from different sides of the country, making the most of all the special moments we were able to spend with each other. After realizing that we would do anything to finally make this relationship work, Frank made the move to California, where we currently live with our white German Shepherd, Enzo.

We are so excited to celebrate the next chapter in our story with all of you, and can’t thank you enough for all the love and support that has already been shown. Please join us on July 29, 2021 in Kirtland, Ohio when we finally make this thing official!
